 2026 MONACO GRAND PRIX 05 – 07 June 2026      From The FIA Formula 1 Media Delegate      To All Officials, All Teams      Date  07 June 2026    NOTE TO TEAMS: PRE-RACE PROCEDURE  Please find below a summary of the requirements for the Pre-Race activities and National Anthem as set out in Article B10.1.4b of the FIA F1 Regulations: “In any case, no less than sixteen (16) minutes before the scheduled start of the formation lap all drivers must be present at the front of the grid for the playing of the national anthem.”  14:40:00 Drivers move to National Anthem Position. Drivers will be expected to be in position no later than 14:42:00.  14:44:00 The National Anthem. For the duration of the National Anthem all Drivers must remain attired only in their race suits.  Failure to comply with these procedures will be reported to the Stewards.  Please see the attached National Anthem Diagram.  Cameron Kelleher  The FIA Formula 1 Media Delegate
